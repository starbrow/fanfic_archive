Fanfic Archive Project

1. Purpose
  a) Create a website/database that can store html + metadata to make an archive for fanfiction.
  b) Allow commenting on individual stories.
  c) Allow some form of likes/kudos/thumbs up.

2. Schedule
  a) By 31 October, start work on the Ruby programming.
  b) By 31 December, have CRUD functionality up and running.
  c) by 31 March, complete site ready for stories to be added.
